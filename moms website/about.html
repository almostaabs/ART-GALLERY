About Me

Hello! I'm AARTI, an artist inspired by nature, people, and imagination. My journey with art began in childhood, and over the years, I've explored a variety of mediums including acrylics, watercolors, and mixed media. Each piece I create is a reflection of my emotions and the world around me.

My work is characterized by vibrant colors, bold brushstrokes, and a deep connection to the stories behind each subject. I believe that art is not just something to be seen, but something to be felt and experienced.

  • Mediums: Acrylics, Watercolors, Mixed Media
  • Favorite Subjects: Landscapes, Portraits, Abstracts
  • Exhibitions: Local galleries, online showcases, and community events
  • Mission: To inspire others and bring joy through art
